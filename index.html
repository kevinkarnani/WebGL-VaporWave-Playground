Camera/Interface Controls

Free Flying Camera
Forward arrow: Move camera forward
Backward arrow: Move camera backward
Right arrow: Move camera right
Left arrow: Move camera left
Z: Roll counterclockwise
z: Roll clockwise
X: Pitch up
x: Pitch down
C: Yaw counterclockwise
c: Yaw clockwise
Car Camera
Forward arrow: Drive car forward
Backward arrow: Drive car backward
Left arrow: Turn car left
Right arrow: Turn car right
Other controls
Spacebar: Switch between free flying camera (default) and car camera
s: Spawn a dolphin (dynamically generated)
